FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

 

  • What do I need to know about the Promisary Note?

    The BMP home buyer will sign to a 20-year Promissory Note for the difference of the homes appraised unrestricted Initial Market Value (IMV) and the restricted Affordable Sales Price (ASP) when the BMP home is initially sold. The Promissory Note is secured by a Deed of Trust and Security Agreement which creates a lien on the home.

  • What if I have not attended the Orientation and Application workshop?

    If you have not attended the Orientation and Application Workshop or the 8 Hour Homebuyer education class, please refer to the Eligibility Requirements.
